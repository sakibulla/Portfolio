# Web3Forms Setup - Simple Contact Form Solution

Web3Forms is the easiest way to get your contact form working. It's free and takes 2 minutes to set up.

## Step 1: Get Your Access Key
1. Go to [web3forms.com](https://web3forms.com)
2. Enter your email: **hasanfahmidul2002@gmail.com**
3. Click "Create Access Key"
4. Copy the access key they give you

## Step 2: Update Your Contact Form
Replace `YOUR_ACCESS_KEY_HERE` in `components/Contact.tsx` with your actual access key:

```typescript
submitData.append('access_key', 'your-actual-access-key-here')
```

## Step 3: Test It
1. Save the file
2. Fill out your contact form
3. Submit it
4. Check hasanfahmidul2002@gmail.com for the email

## How It Works
1. User fills out form on your website
2. Form data is sent to Web3Forms
3. Web3Forms sends you an email at hasanfahmidul2002@gmail.com
4. You can reply directly to the user's email
